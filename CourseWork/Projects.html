United Motors Vehicle Sales Dashboard

June 06, 2025 at 10:59 PM

One of my proudest projects was creating a Vehicle Sales Dashboard for United Motors Sri Lanka, built using Access, MySQL and PowerBI. The goal was to analyze vehicle stock levels, pre-delivery inspections (PDI), and sales performance using real data from RMV and internal inventory sources. I designed the system to provide managers with interactive views, allowing them to filter vehicles by model, status, and sales region helping them make data-driven decisions quickly and efficiently.

Key Features Included:

  • Interactive charts showing monthly and yearly sales trends.
  • Automated summaries of available vs. sold units by model.
  • Linked Access database for structured vehicle information.
  • Dynamic formulas and conditional formatting for visual insights.

Through this project, I learned the importance of data relationships, query optimization, and presentation clarity ensuring that complex datasets become actionable intelligence for business users.

Sustainafy: Building for a Better Tomorrow

February 25, 2025 10.59pm

As part of my Web Design and Development coursework at the University of Westminster, my team and I developed Sustainafy, a website dedicated to Sustainable Cities and Communities, inspired by one of the UN’s Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). We took design inspiration from Samsung’s Galaxy S25 Ultra website — focusing on simplicity, responsiveness, and visual storytelling. My contribution centered on front-end development, where I refined layouts using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, creating an intuitive and elegant interface. This project solidified my love for clean, purposeful design that communicates as effectively as it functions.
